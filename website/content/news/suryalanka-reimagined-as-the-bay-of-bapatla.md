Andhra Pradesh is redeveloping Suryalanka into an international destination branded the ‘Bay of Bapatla’ — with a welcome arch, camping, a cycling-track approach road, cobblestone walkways, water sports, landscaping and an 11 KV solar system. The plan deliberately avoids permanent structures on the sand to limit erosion, and pursues Blue Flag certification with eco-resorts and PPP hotels.
