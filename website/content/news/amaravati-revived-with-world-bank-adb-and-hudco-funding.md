Stalled Amaravati works resumed after the 2024 change of government. In December 2024 the World Bank approved US$800 million and the ADB matched it with US$800 million for the Amaravati Integrated Urban Development Program, alongside ₹11,000 crore from HUDCO. Phase-1 development is estimated at about ₹64,910 crore over three years.
