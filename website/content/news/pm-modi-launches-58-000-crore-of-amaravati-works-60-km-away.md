On 2 May 2025 the Prime Minister laid foundation stones for 74 Amaravati projects worth about ₹49,000 crore (total event value ~₹58,000 crore), covering the Assembly, Secretariat, High Court and housing, plus a ~320 km transport network. Roughly 60 km from the Bapatla coast, the capital's restart is a major regional driver of leisure, business and residential demand.
