Andhra Pradesh's Tourism Policy 2024–2029 prioritises beach, eco and spiritual tourism, planning seven hubs and 25 circuits — including dedicated beach circuits — and granting industry status to hospitality. It targets raising tourism's share of GVA from 4.6% to 8% by 2029 and ₹25,000 crore in private investment, backed by incentives such as 100% stamp-duty reimbursement.
