Bapatla and Chirala railway stations are being upgraded under the Ministry of Railways' Amrit Bharat Station Scheme, improving passenger amenities, accessibility and infrastructure on the busy Vijayawada–Gudur trunk route — easing rail access for visitors to the Suryalanka–Vodarevu coast.
