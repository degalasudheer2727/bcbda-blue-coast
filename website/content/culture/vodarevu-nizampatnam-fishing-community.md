Vodarevu, Chirala and Nizampatnam anchor an active fishing economy on the Bapatla coast, with Nizampatnam a historic seaport known for backwaters and mangroves. These harbours — being upgraded under government fisheries schemes — sustain a long-standing coastal fishing culture that BCBDA treats as co-owners of coastal prosperity.
