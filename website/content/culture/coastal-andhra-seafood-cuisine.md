The Bapatla–Chirala fishing landings feed a fiery seafood table built on fresh prawns, crab and fish. Signature dishes include Chepala Pulusu (tangy tamarind fish curry) and Royyala Vepudu (crisp spiced prawn fry), balancing chilli heat with tamarind sourness in classic Andhra style; dried fish is a regional staple too.
