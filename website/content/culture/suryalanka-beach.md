About 9 km from Bapatla town, Suryalanka is a long stretch of golden sand on the Bay of Bengal known for gentle waves, shallow water and calm sunrise and sunset views — one of the more family-friendly beaches on the Andhra coast. Relatively uncommercialised, it is popular for weekend picnics and photography, with seasonal dolphin-spotting boat trips reported around November.
