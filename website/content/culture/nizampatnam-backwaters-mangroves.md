South of Bapatla on the Krishna-delta fringe, Nizampatnam is known for backwaters, a lighthouse and mangrove forests along the Bay of Bengal. The historic port offers an eco and coastal-nature counterpoint to the district's beaches, part of the region's wider maritime-tourism appeal.
