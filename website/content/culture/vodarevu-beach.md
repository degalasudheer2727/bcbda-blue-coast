Roughly 6–8 km from Chirala, Vodarevu is a coconut-fringed beach popular for boat rides, water sports and its working fishing activity. Promoted as one of the closest beaches to Hyderabad and Telangana, it draws steady weekend crowds and anchors the corridor's marine-recreation node.
