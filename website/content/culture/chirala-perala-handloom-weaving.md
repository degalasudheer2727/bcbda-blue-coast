Chirala, with adjoining Perala, is a historic cotton-weaving town whose Padmasali and Devanga weaver communities are known for soft, durable cotton saris and ‘seico’ cotton-silk blends. Local lore even ties the name ‘Chirala’ to ‘chira’ — sari in Telugu. Handloom is the district's flagship craft, though the cluster has thinned under powerloom competition.
